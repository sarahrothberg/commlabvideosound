COURSE DESCRIPTION

This course explores the fundamentals of sound and video. Students will learn the basics of both audio and video recording using audio field recorders and a variety of cameras (from the Panasonic Xacti through the Canon 5D D-SLR) as well as editing and exporting in Adobe Premiere. Students will work in teams to produce both an audio soundscape and a three-minute video short. This 2-credit course meets for the first seven weeks of the semester. Grading is based on is based on successful completion of all assignments, class journals, class participation and attendance.
